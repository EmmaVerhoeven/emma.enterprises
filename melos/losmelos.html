grupo Los Melos

With band members from the Netherlands, Spain, Venezuela, Italy, Colombia and Germany, Los Melos consists of a mishmash of nationalities, yet they sound as Colombian as formations such as Grupo Niche, Grupo Gale and Curao en Salsa. Expect a band that excels with musical virtuosity, impressive vocal outbursts and a sky-high danceability factor.

music


Percussionist and conguero Nique Quentin released last year A Los Melómanos, an album that brings out the passionate love of music in each of us. It is a true melting pot of cultures, influences and stories from all over the world, just like salsa is! With a place in the Colombian charts and compliments from salsa legend Rubén Blades, the album was also received extremely positively outside the Netherlands.
