3. Oorzaak & gevolg üïµüèª‚Äç‚ôÇÔ∏è

Dus (so, therefore)

Conclusion/result

Ik ben moe, dus ik ga slapen

I am tired, so I am going to sleep

Want (because)

Explanation after the fact

Ik ga slapen, want ik ben moe

I am going to sleep, because I am tired

Omdat (because)

Reason given first; verb switches to the end!

Ik ga slapen, omdat ik moe ben

I am going to sleep, because I am tired

Daarom (that's why)

Referring back to previous statement

Ik ben moe, daarom ga ik slapen

I am tired, that's why I am going to sleep
